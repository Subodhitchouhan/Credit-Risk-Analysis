# Numerical Columns

1.Age: The age of the individual applicant in years.

2.Credit amount: The total sum of money borrowed or requested in the loan.

3.Duration: The length of time (usually in months) over which the loan is to be repaid.

# Categorical Columns- 

4.Sex: The gender of the applicant (Male/Female).

5.Job: The category of the applicant's employment (e.g., unskilled, skilled, management).

6.Housing: Whether the applicant owns, rents, or lives for free.

7.Saving accounts: The level of balance in the applicant's savings (e.g., little, moderate, quite rich).

8.Checking account: The status or balance of the applicant's primary bank account.

9.Purpose: The reason for the loan (e.g., car, furniture, education, business).

10.Risk: The target variable, usually labeled as "Good" or "Bad" credit risk.
